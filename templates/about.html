Service Manual compliance for service.gov.uk domains

The Government Service Design Manual describes in detail some standard that transactional sites on the service.gov.uk domain must satisfy. The requirements are described in detail, however it is difficult to assess immediately if a given transactional site satisfies the requirements.

This simple application takes a transaction start page on GOV.UK and determines whether the transaction it links to satisfies the individual requirements of the Service Manual.
